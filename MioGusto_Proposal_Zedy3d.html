Part 1 — Website Design & Development
Frontend Website (UI)
Agreed

A complete, modern, mobile-friendly restaurant website designed and built from scratch. Includes all pages and features listed below.

Total Pages: 9

  • Page 1 — Home — Hero banner, welcome section, highlights & call to action
  • Page 2 — Menu — Full menu display with categories and prices
  • Page 3 — Gallery — Food and restaurant atmosphere photos
  • Page 4 — Reservations — Customers book a table directly from the website
  • Page 5 — Online Ordering — Order for pickup or delivery
  • Page 7 — Catering — Catering services info and request form
  • Page 8 — Events — Upcoming events and promotions
  • Page 9 — Contact / About — Location, hours, about the restaurant & contact form
$1,000
Part 2 — Admin Panel & Content Management System
Backend Application (CMS)
Proposed

A private, secure admin dashboard accessible only to the owner. No technical knowledge required — log in and manage everything from one place.

  • Secure owner login (username & password)
  • Manage menu — add, edit, or remove items and prices
  • Upload and manage gallery photos
  • Post and manage events
  • Upload flyers and promotions
  • Manage catering information and requests
  • View and manage online reservations and table bookings
  • View and manage online orders
  • Post news and announcements
  • Owner training session included
$3,000
